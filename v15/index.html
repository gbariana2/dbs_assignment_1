What is this?

Campus Coffee Roulette randomly pairs grad students from different Chicago universities for casual, one-on-one coffee chats. No agenda, no pressure — just two people, two coffees, and a conversation that might change your trajectory.

Every two weeks you get a new match. You pick the café, you pick the time. We just make the intro.
