01

What it is

Campus Coffee Roulette pairs you with a fellow graduate student from another Chicago program — someone you would never meet in your own department. No agenda. No pressure. Just coffee and conversation, honoring the Japanese idea of ichigo ichie : one time, one meeting.

Like the wabi-sabi aesthetic, the beauty is in the unplanned — the imperfect encounter that becomes meaningful.
