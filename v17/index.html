The idea

One conversation can
change everything

Graduate school can feel solitary. You spend years immersed in your discipline, surrounded by brilliant people in other programs you may never meet. Campus Coffee Roulette gently changes that.

Each week, we pair one grad student with another from a different program, a different school, a different world of thought. You meet for coffee. That's it. No agenda, no networking pretense — just two curious people sharing an hour.

How it works

Three simple steps

1

Sign up with your university email below. Tell us your name, your school, and your program. That's all we need.

2

Each Monday, you receive an email introducing you to your match — a graduate student from a different program somewhere across Chicago.

3

You find a time, pick a cafe, and meet for coffee. The conversation is yours — we simply open the door.

"The best conversations happen
between people who would never
otherwise meet."
