CAMPUS COFFEE ROULETTE(1) User Commands CAMPUS COFFEE ROULETTE(1)

NAME
coffee-roulette — match Chicago grad students for weekly networking coffee chats

SYNOPSIS
coffee-roulette [--signup] [--match] [--meet] [--repeat]

DESCRIPTION
Campus Coffee Roulette randomly pairs graduate students from different programs and universities across Chicago for one-on-one coffee chats.

Break out of your department silo. Meet brilliant people you'd never cross paths with. All it costs is 30 minutes and one cup of coffee.

New matches drop every Monday at 09:00 CST. 100% free. Zero spam. Just coffee and conversation.

OPTIONS
--cross-program Always matched outside your own department
--cross-university Prioritize matches at different schools
--flexible Meet in-person or virtual — your call
--no-awkward We send conversation starters with every match

$ coffee-roulette --how-it-works
Executing matching algorithm...

[STEP 01] $ ./signup.sh
Sign up with your university email below. Takes 30 seconds. We verify you're actually a grad student (no undergrads, sorry).

[STEP 02] $ cron @weekly ./match.py
Every Monday our algorithm pairs you with someone from a DIFFERENT program or university. Maximum cross-pollination.

[STEP 03] $ mail --notify "Your match is ready!"
You both get an email intro with each other's name, program, and 3 conversation starters. No awkward cold opens.

[STEP 04] $ meet --duration=30m --location=cafe
Grab coffee (or tea, or boba — we don't judge). Chat for 30 minutes. Expand your network across Chicago's grad schools.

[STEP 05] $ goto STEP_02 # loop forever
Next week, new match. Build a network that spans every campus, every discipline, every corner of Chicago academia.

Process complete. Exit code: 0 (SUCCESS)
