The Process

We believe in the quiet power of an unplanned conversation. No algorithms optimizing for engagement. No swiping. Just two graduate students, a shared city, and forty-five minutes.

I.

Register

Submit your name, university email, school, and program. It takes thirty seconds. We verify your .edu address to keep the community trusted and intimate.

II.

Get Matched

Every Monday morning, you receive an email with your match — a grad student from a different program or university. We deliberately cross boundaries.

III.

Meet

Schedule a coffee chat that week. On campus, at a café, or over Zoom. The format is yours. The conversation is unscripted.

Why Join

Break the Silo

Graduate school can be isolating. Your cohort becomes your world. Coffee Roulette introduces you to people thinking about entirely different problems — and that’s where the most interesting ideas live.

Build a Cross-Disciplinary Network

The lawyer who understands machine learning. The MBA who studied bioethics. The urban planner who minored in philosophy. These intersections create careers that don’t exist on any job board.

Practice the Art of Conversation

In an age of curated online personas, sitting across from a stranger and having a genuine exchange is a radical act. It’s also a skill — one that compounds over a lifetime.

Join the Table

Registration is simple and takes under a minute. We’ll verify your university email, then you’ll receive your first match the following Monday.

What we ask

Your name, a valid .edu email, your university, and your current program. Nothing more.

What we don’t

We don’t sell data. We don’t spam. We don’t share your information beyond your weekly match. This is a community built on trust.
