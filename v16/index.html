01

What This Is

Campus Coffee Roulette pairs graduate students from different Chicago universities for one-on-one coffee conversations. No agendas, no networking scripts, no elevator pitches. Just two people from different programs, meeting over a warm drink, discovering what they didn't know they had in common.

Each month, you'll receive a single match — a name, a program, and a suggestion to meet. What happens after that is entirely up to you. Some matches become collaborators. Some become friends. All of them become a reminder that the best ideas live at the edges between disciplines.

02

How It Works

Sign up

Share your name, your university, and the program you're in. That's all we need. No profiles, no bios, no photos.

Get matched

On the first Monday of each month, you'll receive an email introducing you to someone from a different graduate program at a different university.

Meet for coffee

Find a time that week. Pick a café roughly between your campuses. Sit down for thirty minutes. That's it — no structure, no follow-up survey.

Repeat or rest

Stay in the pool for next month's match, or pause whenever you like. There is no obligation, only invitation.
